Phishing detection helper


















        Think before you click

        Never click a link you are not fully confident about - take a moment to verify it first.

        Check suspicious links

        Before opening any link, verify it using URLVoid - a free tool that helps determine whether a link is safe and reputable.

        Protect your devices with a firewall

        Install a firewall on all your devices, including your phone. We recommend Ugunsmuris by CERT.LV - a free DNS firewall that blocks malicious websites automatically.

        Keep your personal data safe

        Never enter personal data on a website you do not fully trust - when in doubt, close the page.
